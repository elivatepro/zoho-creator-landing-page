The problem

Spreadsheets weren't built to run your operations

01

Version chaos

Five people editing the same Google Sheet. Nobody knows which version is current. Data gets overwritten weekly.

02

Zero access control

Everyone sees everything: pricing, salaries, client notes. There's no way to limit who sees what.

03

Manual everything

Copy-pasting between tabs, sending manual follow-ups, exporting CSVs. Hours lost to work a machine should do.

04

No real reporting

You're making decisions on gut feeling because pulling actual numbers takes half a day of pivot tables.

What changes when you ditch the spreadsheet

Now
  • Team overwrites each other's data
  • No audit trail: who changed what?
  • Clients email you for status updates
  • Reporting takes hours of manual work
  • New hires take weeks to learn "the sheet"
→
After
  • Role-based access: everyone sees only their data
  • Full change history on every record
  • Client portal with real-time status view
  • Live dashboards that update automatically
  • Onboarding is "here's your login"
